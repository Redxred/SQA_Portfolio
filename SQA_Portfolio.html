About

Quality is not only the absence of bugs; it is predictable behavior under real-world conditions.

Testing philosophy

Start with the user journey and the business risk, then build coverage through boundary analysis, state transitions, and a deliberate mix of positive and negative scenarios. Keep artifacts small, readable, and traceable.

Quality signals

Stable CI, low flaky rate, clear release notes, meaningful metrics, and a defect curve that tells a coherent story (not a scary one). If the metric can be gamed, it will be.
